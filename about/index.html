About us.

TEMBUK LLC Coaching Services is a new life coaching business in Greater Manchester, United Kingdom. Our goal is to help individuals and families find purpose and direction in their life and make positive changes to reach their goals. Our team of experienced coaches offers a variety of services, from helping individuals develop self-confidence and improve their communication skills to providing couples counseling and family therapy. We also provide business coaching services, helping entrepreneurs create and implement strategies to meet their business objectives.At TEMBUK LLC Coaching Services, we strive to provide high-quality and personalized services to each of our clients. We believe that every individual and family has unique needs and goals, and we are committed to helping them find the best path to success. Our coaches are available to discuss your goals and provide guidance and support throughout your journey. We look forward to helping you make the changes you need to get to where you want to be.
